ABOUT US

ProjectAnalysis is a professional trading journal which supports traders with tracking and analyzing their trades. Our goal is to create a platform which not only consists of a web application but also a community, where knowledge can be shared, and groups can be formed. We are aiming to create a simple yet effective tool which enhances your trading experience by a lot. Through an automated trade import, we have achieved to design a beginner friendly and easy environment. Spotting personal patterns and mistakes thereby becomes very easy and straight forward.
